Why Does vLoop Exist?

Because doing anything with a video online besides just looking it at is impossible or a huge pain in the ass for most people.

Integration and Workflow Automation

Video is the last relic of the old internet. It stands alone in a silo. We bring the power of video into the tools you use every day.

  • Webhooks to export clips directly to platforms like Slack and Salesforce so people can view video inside the solutions they already use.

  • Webhooks to import data that is related to a video for analysis purposes. (ie sales data connected to video of sales pitches).

  • Use video annotations and tagging to coach performance in sales and presentation settings.

  • Easily add technology layers like facial recognition, audio to text, computer vision onto the video for enhanced value.

Video is great tool to drive performance improvement. We make it a more natural part of your workflow and let you take advantage of other video technology without any software development required.

The new "comment"

Our technology extends beyond our platform. Comments revolutionized the way people interact with content on the internet by providing them a way to actively engage rather than as a passive consumer. Our vision is to become for video what the "comment" has been for content. vLoop can provide users new and active ways to interact with a video.

A phased approach

Our strategy focuses on a phased approach to development. We have initially invested significant time and resources in the core infrastructure required to handle a wide array of video processes and operations in a fast and cost effective way. We have also focused on building a viable sports business standalone.

Each applicable industry that can benefit from video has differences in terms of UI design and user experience, but all rely on the same underlying technology and video functionality. We are focused on executing on a market specific basis to ensure we deliver the best possible product with a relevant marketing message.

Why sports first?

Building new video technology is hard. It requires serious infrastructure and engineeering to create any meaningful value. Our belief is that the process of innovation is most effective in an iterative and test drive environment.

Our team has significant background in sports (former college and professional athletes) and it represents the fastest market to drive meaningful product feedback and revenue growth. The economics of the sports market also enable us to more quickly pursue profitabiltiy.

We have been able to rapdily iterate on the core technology and get it to a performance level that will allow us to soon expand to other markets with greater market size and increased revenue per customer but also longer sales cycles.
