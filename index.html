The Science of Improvement

Rethink the process of video analysis for teams

Deliberate Practice

The concept of deliberate practice was popularized by the Malcolm Gladwell book Outliers. Scientific evidence strongly supports the notion that building a customized training regimen focused around specific needs is far superior to more generic practice routines. vLoop's coding & collaboration features make it easy to clearly show areas for improvement and communicate a solution.

Data Driven Analysis

Professional sports teams that embrace data have seen a huge advantage over the past decade. The usage of statistics in performance analysis is proven to deliver faster results. vLoop offers you the power of professional grade analytics tools without a huge price tag.

Active Learning

A large body of scientific research suggests the process of asking questions rather than only providing answers is the most effective method for getting students to learn. vLoop's collaboration tools are built with this principle in mind.

Measurable Progress

People improve faster with clearly defined goals and measurable ways to see progress. vLoop's elegant design makes this easy.
